The $n$-cube is a [[Graphs|graph]] that has $2^{n}$ vertices and is given by $Q_{n}=(V_{n},E_{n})$, $V_{n}=\{ x:\{ 1,2,\dots,n \}\to \{ 0,1 \} \}$ $E_{n}=\left\{  uv:\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left| u_{i}-v_{i} \right|=1  \right\}$
