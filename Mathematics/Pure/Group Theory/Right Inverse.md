Let $(G,*)$ be a [[groups|group]] with element $b\in G$
$$
b*b^{-1}=1
$$
## Proof
$$
b*b^{-1}=1*(b*b^{-1})
$$
$$
\dots = ((b^{-1})^{-1}*b^{-1})*(b*b^{-1})
$$
$$
\dots = (((b^{-1})^{-1}*b^{-1})*b)*b^{-1}
$$
$$
\dots = ((b^{-1})^{-1}*(b^{-1}*b))*b^{-1}
$$
$$
\dots = ((b^{-1})^{-1}*1)*b^{-1}
$$
$$
\dots = (b^{-1})^{-1}*b^{-1}
$$
$$
\dots = 1
$$
