The nullspace of a [[Matrices|matrix]] $A\in M_{n\times k}(\mathbb{R})$ is the space of solutions to $A\vec{x}=\vec{0}$;
$$
\text{nullspace}(A)=\{ \vec{x}\in \mathbb{R}^{k}\mid A\vec{x}= \vec{0} \}
$$
