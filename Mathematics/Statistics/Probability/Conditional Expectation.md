$$
\mathbb{1}_{A}(\omega)=1
$$
From this:
$$
E(\mathbb{1}_{A})=1\times \mathbb{P}(A)+0\times \mathbb{P}(A^{c})=\mathbb{P}(A)
$$
So the conditional expectation of $X$ given $A$, is
$$
E(X|A):=\frac{E(X \mathbb{1}_{A})}{\mathbb{P}(A)}
